How to pursue Indian Bachelors in CSE Online for free

Nov 28, 2020

Computer Science Engineering the Buzzword priority number one for most of Indian students and under graduate engineering students.Why not, Computer and Internet are basic necessities. Majority of non-medical students want to pursue Bachelors in CSE and under-graduate Masters in CSE TA, not everybody wants Masters in CSE RA.

Today in the world of internet open education is the new norm. But do we really need to go college for CSE degree, majorly and commonly CSE degrees in India are about DSA.
Nothing wrong, but Computer Science Engineering is the study of computer which covers theoretical area of computer science with practical are and ratio of areas undefined.

Today anybody can become Software Engineer, Data Scientist, ML Engineer with relevant skills. Programs like neogcamp, appliedaicourse and more had open path for CS-role related careers for everybody.

What you need to know

It is not even close to offerings by NITs, BITs, IITs, IISC and such institutes. Reffer this as Engineering Bachelors TA.

Learning methodology to keep

How were going to be applied: appreciate, connect dots and real world. examples: grep command in unix, auto complete on google, facebook friend recommendation.

Where to start ?

If today i have to pursue under-grad in CSE. I need computer, interent, resources and Guru. Yes Guru to discuss.
Arranged on the basis of syllabus, time and practice to get started and appreciate. It is a two phase strategy,

  • Phase1: Studying books and solving examples (125 days, 8 hours/day).

    Syllabus topics Book Name Author Name Days to be Devoted Relevant Chapters
    TOC An Introduction to Formal Languages and Automata Peter Linz 7 1-12
    CD Compilers: Principles, Techniques and Tools Aho & Ullman 6 1-9
    DLD Digital Design Morris Manno 6 1-6
    Discrete Mathematics Discrete Mathematics and Its Applications Kenneth Rosen 20 1,2,6-11
    Data structures & Algorithms Introduction to Algorithms Cormen 14 1-4,6-8,10-12, 15,16,22-24,34
    Database Database System Concepts Korth 12 1-4,6-8,10, 11,14,15
    Operating System Operating System Concepts Galvin 14 1-15
    Computer Networks Computer Networking: A Top-Down Approach Kurose & Ross 10 1-5,8
    Computer Organization Computer Organization and Embedded systems Carl Hamacher 5 1-3,5,6,8,9

    Don not spend More than 20 Days on a Subject.

  • Phase2: Notes, Revision and solving practice papers (30-60 days, 12-8 hours/day).

    For notes, refer gate.appliedroots as they save time and include relevant material with sample practice questions.
    For Practice papers solve PYQ's of GATE CS Entrance Exam, for that visit gateoverflow.

Thus, this is how one can pursue CS degree for free online, make sure to find Guru to discuss
